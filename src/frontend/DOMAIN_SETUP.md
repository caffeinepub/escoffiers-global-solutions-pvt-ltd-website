# Connecting escoffiers.com to Internet Computer

This guide explains how to connect your purchased domain `escoffiers.com` to your Internet Computer frontend canister without using third-party hosting services.

## Overview

The Internet Computer allows you to host your frontend directly on-chain and connect custom domains via DNS configuration. No external hosting (AWS, Vercel, Netlify, etc.) is required.

## Prerequisites

1. Access to your domain registrar's DNS management panel for `escoffiers.com`
2. Your deployed frontend canister ID
3. The boundary node domain assigned to your canister
